About Us

Brenda Smith has over 40 years of sewing experience. She was a volunteer costume mistress for a pre- professional dance company for many years. She works closely with the dancers each year, to be sure the costumes fit perfectly, before performances. Her passion is making Classical ballet tutus. She had an opportunity to take Tutu construction class with a Designer from Pacific Northwest Ballet in 2007. She also enjoys making historic ball gown replications, party dresses, and other Nutcracker ballet costumes.

Nicole Conley, designer, has many years of ballet training and understands the needs and wants of dancers and how their costumes should fit and feel. She has a BA in Apparel design and loves to design tutus and special occasion dresses.

In recent years, Brenda has expanded her repertoire to include professional alteration and creation of womens bridal wear.

Special awards include first prize in Tutu.com's national Tutu contest in 2010, with one of her Black Swan tutus.

She was also a winner of Threads Magazine 2014 Fancy Fabric Challenge, with a recent Sugar Plum Tutu that she created.
