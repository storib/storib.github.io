About the Founder

Brenda Smith has over 40 years of sewing experience. She has been the volunteer costume mistress for pre-professional dance company, Columbia Dance, since 2005. She specializes in classical tutus, historic ball gown replications, and other dance costumes.

In recent years, she has expanded her repertoire to include professional alteration and creation of bridal gowns and women's formalwear.

Special awards include first prize in Tutu.com's annual national tutu contest in 2010.
